Our Story

GamerXise was created out of necessity by a founder with ADHD and a TBI survivor who was suffering from chronic migraines. Struggling to maintain healthy exercise habits, he turned to what he knew worked: the immersive world of video games. By merging video games and exercise, he found a way to improve his health and happiness. GamerXise is now a solution designed to benefit the whole world, offering full-body, strength, and cardio workouts that can be done from the comfort of your living room, with no additional equipment required.

Our Services

GamerXise Monthly

A monthly subscription providing new workouts every month. Get guided workouts and resources to make exercising while gaming easy, along with educational resources to teach you the basics and bonuses to improve your results.

GamerXise Client

For those with specific goals or needing a tailored program, GamerXise Client offers a personal trainer to assess, guide, create, and monitor your program, adjusting it as needed. Ideal for game skill goals, health challenges, or specific physical targets.

GamerXise Programming

Get a semi-personalized program designed by a personal trainer, with monthly updates and adjustments. This option includes an initial assessment and program fee, followed by a lower monthly cost for unlimited updates.
